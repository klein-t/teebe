## How to sign

For now, signing is by **explicit statement in your first pull request**. Add a
comment to the PR containing exactly:

> I have read and agree to the Teebe Contributor License Agreement (CLA.md).

and include a `Signed-off-by:` line in your commits:

```
Signed-off-by: Your Name <your.email@example.com>
```

(produced automatically by `git commit -s`). One signed statement covers all of
your future contributions to this project.
